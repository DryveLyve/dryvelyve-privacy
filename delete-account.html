Delete Your DryveLyve Account

Effective Date: May 24, 2026
Last Updated: May 24, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your DryveLyve account and associated data. DryveLyve is a mileage tracking app for gig drivers, developed by DryveLyve Support based in Spring Hill, Tennessee, United States.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you cannot recover it. If you wish to use DryveLyve again, you will need to create a new account.

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1 — In the App (Recommended)

If you have the DryveLyve app installed and can sign in, this is the fastest method:

  1. Open the DryveLyve app on your Android device.
  2. Navigate to the Subscription screen (accessible from the dashboard subscription badge or from Settings).
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion in the confirmation dialog.

The deletion is processed immediately. You will be returned to the email-verification screen.

Method 2 — By Email Request

If you no longer have the app installed, cannot sign in, or prefer to delete by email:

  1. Send an email to dryvelyve.supp0rt@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include the email address associated with your DryveLyve account in the body of the email.

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm by reply email once deletion is complete.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account (email address and hashed password)
  • Your Cloud Firestore trial-eligibility ledger entry (SHA-256 hash of your email and the email itself)
  • Your local subscription record on the device (if signed in via the in-app method)

What Is NOT Automatically Deleted

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and is not affected by account deletion:

  • Trip records (waypoints, distances, durations)
  • Vehicle records and odometer history
  • Expense records (fuel, maintenance, and other categories)
  • Receipt photos
  • Earnings, platform performance, and custom categories
  • App settings and language preferences

To remove this local data, uninstall the DryveLyve app from your device. Uninstalling permanently deletes all locally stored data unless Android Auto Backup is enabled in your device settings, in which case some data may be restored if you reinstall the app on the same Google account.

Retention Period

  • Firebase Authentication account: Deleted immediately upon request.
  • Firestore trial-ledger entry: Deleted immediately upon request.
  • Google's internal backup snapshots: Up to 30 days, then permanently deleted by Google. DryveLyve does not have access to or control over these backups.

DryveLyve does not operate its own servers. There is no manual retention of your data outside of Google's Firebase platform.

After Deletion

After your account is deleted, you may create a new DryveLyve account at any time using:

  • The same email address, or
  • A different email address

Note that Google Play enforces a one-trial-per-Google-account policy independent of DryveLyve. The same Google account cannot start a second free trial regardless of which email address is used to create a new DryveLyve account.
