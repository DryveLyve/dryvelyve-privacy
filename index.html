DryveLyve Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Introduction

DryveLyve is a mileage tracking app that collects and processes location data locally on your device to automatically track your driving trips for mileage logging and business expense tracking.

Key Privacy Principle: All data collected by DryveLyve is stored and processed locally on your device. Your trip data, location history, and personal information are never transmitted externally. This policy explains how your device permissions are used for local processing only.

What Data We Collect and How We Use It

Location Data (Collected): DryveLyve collects and processes your GPS coordinates and trip routes to calculate mileage and track your driving trips. Location data is collected continuously while the app is running in the background. If you swipe the app off your screen, it will continue running in the background and collecting data. However, if you force close the app through your device settings, background data collection will stop until you reopen the app. All location data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Activity Recognition Data (Collected): DryveLyve collects motion and activity data from your device sensors to detect when you start and stop driving. This helps prevent false trip triggers and ensures accurate trip detection.

Device Information (Collected): DryveLyve collects basic device information such as device manufacturer, model, operating system version, and app version. This information is used solely for error logging and troubleshooting purposes, and is only sent to DryveLyve if you choose to submit a crash report or feedback via email.

What Data We Do NOT Collect or Transmit

• No External Transmission: Your GPS coordinates, trip routes, and location data never leave your device

• No Personal Information: DryveLyve does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifying information

• No Financial Data Transmission: Expense records, mileage rates, and earnings calculations stay on your device

• No User Accounts: The app requires no registration, login, or user profile

• No Analytics: DryveLyve does not use analytics services that track your behavior or app usage

App Functionality

DryveLyve is designed to run continuously (24/7) in the background to automatically detect and track driving trips. When you swipe the app off your screen, it remains active in the background. To completely stop background tracking, you must force close the app through your device settings or revoke location permissions. All data processing occurs locally on your device with no external transmission of your trip, location, or expense data.

Optional Crash Reporting and User Feedback

DryveLyve includes optional features that allow you to send crash reports and feedback directly to the developer:

How It Works:

• User Control: If the app crashes, you will be prompted with an option to send a crash report via your email app. This is entirely optional and requires your explicit action.

• Email-Based: Crash reports are sent through your device's email app (Gmail, Outlook, etc.) - you can review the entire report before sending.

• No Automatic Transmission: Nothing is sent automatically. You must manually choose to send the report.

• Feedback Button: The app includes a "Send Feedback" option in Settings that allows you to voluntarily email the developer with questions, issues, or suggestions.

What Crash Reports Contain:

• Device manufacturer and model (e.g., "Samsung Galaxy S21")

• Android version (e.g., "Android 13")

• App version number

• Crash timestamp

• Technical error details (stack trace)

What Crash Reports Do NOT Contain:

• No GPS coordinates or location data

• No trip history or mileage records

• No expense amounts or financial information

• No photos or receipt images

• No personal identifying information

Developer Use of Crash Reports: Crash reports you voluntarily send are used solely to identify and fix bugs, improve app stability and performance, and respond to user-reported issues. DryveLyve does not share, sell, or distribute crash report information to any third parties.

Device Permissions Explained

The following permissions are used by the app. Each permission is used only for local data processing:

Location Permissions (Required)

• Precise Location (GPS): Required to track trip routes and calculate mileage accurately

• Background Location: Required for automatic trip detection while the app runs in the background

• Usage: Location data is processed locally to record trips and never transmitted externally

Activity Recognition (Required)

• Purpose: Detects whether you are stationary, walking, or driving

• Usage: Prevents false trip detection and improves accuracy of automatic trip start/stop

• Local Only: Activity data is processed on-device and never transmitted

Notifications (Required)

• Purpose: Shows persistent notification during trip tracking and alerts for subscription status

• Usage: Required by Android for background location services

• Control: You can customize notification settings in your device settings

Camera (Optional)

• Purpose: Capture receipt photos for expense tracking

• Usage: Photos are stored locally on your device only

• Control: You can deny this permission and manually select photos from your gallery instead

Storage/Photos (Optional)

• Purpose: Access photos from your gallery for receipt attachments and export reports

• Usage: Read access for selecting existing photos; write access for saving exported reports

• Control: You can deny this permission and use camera capture only

Internet (Required)

• Purpose: Verify subscription status with Google Play Billing

• Usage: Only communicates with Google Play for subscription verification

• No Data Transmission: Your trip data, location data, and personal information are never transmitted over the internet

Data Storage

• Local Storage Only: All trip data, expense records, and settings are stored exclusively on your device

• Database: Data is stored in a local SQLite database within the app's private storage

• Photos: Receipt images are stored in the app's private directory on your device

• No Cloud Sync: DryveLyve does not sync data to any cloud services

• Uninstall: All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

Android Auto Backup

DryveLyve supports Android's Auto Backup feature, which is controlled by your device settings:

• What's Backed Up: If enabled in your device settings, Android may back up your app data (trips, expenses, settings) to your personal Google account

• Your Control: This backup is managed by Android system settings, not by DryveLyve

• Encryption: Android Auto Backup data is encrypted and stored in your personal Google account

• Restoration: When you install DryveLyve on a new device or reinstall the app, Android may automatically restore your data. You will be prompted to keep or clear this restored data

• Opt-Out: You can disable this feature in your device's backup settings at any time

Subscription and Payment Processing

• Google Play Billing: All subscription and payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play Store

• Subscription Verification: The app only verifies your subscription status with Google Play Billing - it does not transmit your personal information, trip data, or location data

• No Payment Data: DryveLyve never sees or stores your credit card information, billing address, or payment details

• Anonymous: DryveLyve cannot identify individual subscribers or link subscription payments to specific devices

Third-Party Services

• Google Play Services: Used for subscription verification and Activity Recognition API

• Android Auto Backup: System-level backup to your personal Google account (controlled by your device settings)

• No Analytics: DryveLyve does not use third-party analytics services

• No Advertising: DryveLyve does not use advertising networks or tracking pixels

• No Social Media: DryveLyve does not integrate with social media platforms

Children's Privacy

DryveLyve is designed for users who are 18 years of age or older who perform gig work or need to track vehicle mileage for business or personal purposes. DryveLyve does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 years of age. Since all data remains stored locally on the user's device, DryveLyve has no access to any user data regardless of age.

International Users

DryveLyve is currently available only in the United States. Since all data processing occurs locally on your device with no external transmission, there are no international data transfers or cross-border data flows. Your data never leaves your device regardless of your location.

Data Security Recommendations

Since your data is stored exclusively on your device, protecting your device protects your data:

• Enable device encryption (automatic on most modern Android devices)

• Use a strong screen lock (PIN, pattern, password, or biometric)

• Enable remote device wipe capabilities (Find My Device)

• Export and backup data regularly using the in-app export feature

• Keep your device OS and apps updated

• Report lost or stolen devices to your carrier immediately
