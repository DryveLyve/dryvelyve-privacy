DryveLyve Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 24, 2026
Last Updated: May 24, 2026

Introduction

DryveLyve is a mileage tracking app for gig drivers. It uses GPS, motion sensors, and Google Play services to automatically detect and record driving trips for tax and business expense purposes.

Local-First for Your Content: All trip data, location history, expense records, vehicle records, receipt photos, and reports are stored exclusively on your device. None of that ever leaves your device.

Cloud Services for Your Account: Starting with version 10, DryveLyve uses Google's Firebase services to manage your account and verify subscription eligibility. Only your email address and a one-way hash of your email are stored in the cloud — never your trip data or financial information.

What We Collect

1. Trip and Vehicle Data (Stored Locally Only)

  • GPS coordinates and trip waypoints
  • Trip start and end times, distance, duration
  • Vehicle odometer readings and history
  • Expense records (fuel, maintenance, other)
  • Receipt photos
  • Earnings, platform performance, and custom categories

GPS coordinates and motion data are continuously processed on your device to automatically detect when trips start and end. Background location polling runs at a reduced interval between trips to conserve battery. This data is stored in a private SQLite database within your app's private directory. It is never transmitted to DryveLyve servers or any third party, and is never used for advertising, profiling, analytics, or any purpose unrelated to mileage tracking.

2. Account Data (Stored on Firebase Authentication)

  • Email address you provide during signup
  • Password (hashed by Firebase; DryveLyve never has access to the plaintext)
  • Firebase User ID (assigned by Google)
  • Email verification status

This data is used solely to identify you when you sign in, send a verification email, allow password reset, and link your account to your subscription.

3. Trial Eligibility Ledger (Stored on Cloud Firestore)

  • SHA-256 hash of your lowercased email address
  • Your email address (used for ownership verification by Firestore security rules)

This is used to enforce a one-trial-per-email rule across reinstalls and account changes. The SHA-256 hash is one-way and cannot be reversed to retrieve your email.

4. App Integrity Verification (Transient)

  • Tokens from Google's Play Integrity API confirming your app installation is genuine

These tokens are generated on demand when sensitive operations occur. They are not stored by DryveLyve and are not linked to your personal data.

5. Device Information (Only with Crash Reports You Send)

  • Device manufacturer and model
  • Android version
  • App version
  • Crash timestamp and technical error details

This information is sent only if you explicitly choose to submit a crash report or feedback via your device's email app. Crash reports contain no GPS coordinates, trip history, expense amounts, photos, or other personal data.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • GPS coordinates or trip data is never sent off your device.
  • No names, phone numbers, addresses, or other identifying information beyond your email.
  • No expense amounts, earnings, or financial transactions are transmitted externally.
  • No third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, etc.).
  • No advertising networks or tracking pixels.
  • No social media integrations.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any party.

Third-Party Services

DryveLyve uses the following Google services:

  • Firebase Authentication — account creation, sign-in, password reset, email verification
  • Cloud Firestore — trial eligibility ledger
  • Firebase App Check (Play Integrity provider) — fraud prevention
  • Google Play Integrity API — app installation verification
  • Google Play Billing — subscription processing and verification
  • Google Play Services (Location and Activity Recognition) — runs on-device; no trip data transmitted
  • Android Auto Backup — system-level backup to your personal Google account (controlled by your device settings)

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

DryveLyve does NOT use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or marketing platforms.

Device Permissions Explained

Location Permissions (Required)

  • Precise Location (GPS): Required to detect trip start and stop points and record trip waypoints for mileage calculation
  • Background Location: Required for automatic trip detection while the app runs in the background
  • Local Processing: All location data is processed and stored locally on your device and is never transmitted externally

Activity Recognition (Required)

  • Purpose: Detects whether you are stationary, walking, or driving
  • Usage: Prevents false trip detection and improves accuracy
  • Local Only: Activity data is processed on-device and never transmitted

Notifications (Required)

  • Purpose: Persistent notification during trip tracking and alerts for subscription status
  • Usage: Required by Android for background location services

Camera (Optional)

  • Purpose: Capture receipt photos for expense tracking
  • Local Only: Photos are stored locally on your device

Storage/Photos (Optional)

  • Purpose: Access photos from your gallery and save exported reports
  • Control: You can deny this permission and use camera capture only

Internet (Required)

  • Purpose: Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Play Integrity, Google Play Billing
  • No Trip Data Transmission: Your trip data, location history, expense records, and photos are never transmitted over the internet

Data Storage and Retention

Local Storage (Your Device)

  • All trip data, expenses, vehicles, photos, and settings are stored in a private SQLite database in your app's private directory
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes this data (unless Android Auto Backup is enabled, in which case it is backed up to your personal Google account)

Cloud Storage (Google)

  • Firebase Auth account data is retained while your account exists
  • Firestore trial-ledger entry is retained while your account exists
  • Both are permanently deleted when you delete your account through the in-app or online deletion option
  • Data is stored in Google's United States data centers

No DryveLyve Servers: DryveLyve does not operate its own servers. All cloud-stored data is held by Google Cloud / Firebase.

Account Deletion

You may delete your DryveLyve account at any time:

  • In the App: Open the Subscription screen → tap "Delete Account"
  • Online: Visit https://dryvelyve.github.io/dryvelyve-privacy/delete-account.html

Account deletion removes:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account (email and password hash)
  • Your Firestore trial-ledger entry
  • Your local subscription record on the device

Account deletion does NOT automatically remove your local trip data, expense records, vehicle records, or photos. To remove these, uninstall the app.

Subscription and Payment Processing

  • Google Play Billing: All subscription and payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play Store
  • 30-Day Free Trial: New subscribers receive a 30-day free trial, managed by Google Play. One trial per Google account is enforced by Play; one trial per email address is enforced by the Firestore trial ledger.
  • No Payment Data: DryveLyve never sees or stores your credit card information, billing address, or payment details
  • Verification: Subscription status is verified periodically with Google Play Billing

Children's Privacy

DryveLyve is intended for adults (18 years of age or older) who track mileage for gig work, business, or personal purposes. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Availability and International Users

DryveLyve is currently available in the United States and Canada. Cloud-stored account data is held in Google's United States data centers. For users outside the US, account data may be subject to international transfer, governed by Google's Standard Contractual Clauses and Cross-Border Privacy Rules.

Your Privacy Rights

  • Access: Local data is accessible via the app. Cloud-stored data (your email and verification status) is displayed in the app's Subscription screen.
  • Export: Export local trip and expense data as CSV or Excel from the in-app export feature.
  • Deletion: Use in-app or online account deletion to remove all cloud data. Uninstall to remove local data.
  • Withdraw Consent: Sign out of your account at any time. Delete your account to revoke processing of your email.

DryveLyve was designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • Trip data, location history, and personal financial data remain on your device

  • No analytics, no advertising, no behavioral tracking
  • Full data sovereignty over your trip content
  • Account deletion available at any time, both in-app and online

This Privacy Policy provides complete transparency about DryveLyve's data practices and your control over your personal information.
