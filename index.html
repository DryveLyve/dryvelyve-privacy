Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 5, 2025

Introduction

DryveLyve is a mileage tracking app developed by Nicholas Hudson ("we", "us", or "our") that collects and processes location data locally on your device to automatically track your driving trips for mileage logging and business expense tracking.

Key Privacy Principle: All data collected by DryveLyve is stored and processed locally on your device. We do not transmit, upload, or have access to your trip data, location history, or personal information on external servers. This policy explains how your device permissions are used for local processing only.

What Data We Collect and How We Use It

Location Data (Collected): We collect and process your GPS coordinates and trip routes to calculate mileage and track your driving trips. This location data is collected continuously in the background, even when the app is closed or not in use. All location data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates and trip routes are collected continuously in the background to automatically detect and record driving trips. This data is processed and stored exclusively on your device for mileage calculation and trip tracking.
  • Activity Recognition Data: Motion and activity data from your device sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope) is collected to detect when you start and stop driving. This helps prevent false trip triggers and ensures accurate trip detection. All processing occurs locally.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as operating system version and app version is collected for functionality purposes. This data stays on your device.
  • Trip Records: Start/end times, locations, distances, durations, and trip classifications (business/personal) are stored locally on your device.
  • Expense Records: Amounts, categories, dates, descriptions, and receipt photos are stored locally on your device.

What Data We Do NOT Collect or Transmit

  • No External Transmission: Your GPS coordinates, trip routes, and location data never leave your device or get transmitted to our servers
  • No Personal Information Collection on Servers: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifying information on external servers
  • No Financial Data Transmission: Expense records, mileage rates, and earnings calculations stay on your device
  • No User Accounts: The app requires no registration, login, or user profile
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics services that track your behavior or app usage

App Functionality

DryveLyve runs continuously (24/7) in the background to automatically detect and track driving trips. All data processing occurs locally on your device with no external transmission of your trip, location, or expense data.

Optional Crash Reporting and User Feedback

DryveLyve includes optional features that allow you to send crash reports and feedback directly to the developer:

How It Works

  • User Control: When the app crashes, you will be prompted with an option to send a crash report via your email app. This is entirely optional and requires your explicit action.
  • Email-Based: Crash reports are sent through your device's email app (Gmail, Outlook, etc.) - you can review the entire report before sending.
  • No Automatic Transmission: Nothing is sent automatically. You must manually choose to send the report.
  • Feedback Button: The app includes a "Send Feedback" button in Settings that allows you to voluntarily email the developer with questions, issues, or suggestions.

What Crash Reports Contain

If you choose to send a crash report, it includes only technical diagnostic information:

  • Device manufacturer and model (e.g., "Samsung Galaxy S21")
  • Android version (e.g., "Android 13")
  • App version number
  • Crash timestamp
  • Technical error details (stack trace)

What Crash Reports Do NOT Contain

  • No GPS coordinates or location data
  • No trip history or mileage records
  • No expense amounts or financial information
  • No photos or receipt images
  • No personal identifying information

Developer Use of Crash Reports

Crash reports you voluntarily send are used solely to:

  • Identify and fix bugs in the app
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Respond to user-reported issues

The developer (Nicholas Hudson) does not share, sell, or distribute crash report information to any third parties.

Device Permissions Explained

The following permissions are required for the app to function. All permissions are used exclusively for local processing on your device:

Location Permission (Required)

  • Purpose: Track GPS coordinates to automatically detect and record driving trips
  • Usage: Continuous background tracking to monitor your movements and detect when you start/stop driving, even when the app is closed or not in use
  • Storage: Location data is stored only on your device in the app's local database
  • Privacy: Your location data never leaves your device

Activity Recognition Permission (Required)

  • Purpose: Distinguish between walking, driving, and being stationary to prevent false trip triggers
  • Usage: Reduces battery consumption by only activating GPS when driving activity is detected
  • Privacy: Activity data (still, walking, in vehicle) is processed locally and never transmitted

Camera Permission (Optional)

  • Purpose: Scan receipts to extract expense information automatically
  • Usage: Only activated when you manually choose to scan a receipt
  • Storage: Receipt images are stored locally on your device
  • Privacy: Photos never leave your device

Storage Permission (Required)

  • Purpose: Save trip records, expense data, and receipt images to your device's internal storage
  • Usage: Read/write app data, export reports as CSV/PDF files
  • Privacy: All files remain on your device

Notification Permission (Required)

  • Purpose: Display trip status, tracking alerts, and mileage goal notifications
  • Usage: Keep you informed about trip detection and app status
  • Privacy: Notifications are displayed locally on your device only

Boot Permission (Required)

  • Purpose: Automatically restart trip tracking when your device reboots
  • Usage: Ensures continuous tracking without manual intervention
  • Privacy: No data is accessed or transmitted during boot

Local Data Storage

All app data is stored exclusively on your device using:

  • Room Database: Trip records, expenses, vehicle information, and user settings
  • Internal Storage: Receipt images and exported reports
  • SharedPreferences: App configuration and preferences

Important: If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted from your device. We cannot recover your data because we never had access to it.

Third-Party Services

DryveLyve uses Google Play Billing for subscription management:

  • Google Play Billing: Processes in-app subscription purchases securely through Google's payment system
  • What Google Collects: Payment information, purchase history, and subscription status are handled by Google Play according to Google's Privacy Policy
  • What We Receive: We only receive confirmation that your subscription is active/inactive - no payment details

We do not use any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your data with anyone because:

  • We do not have access to your data (it stays on your device)
  • We do not operate servers or cloud storage
  • We do not collect user analytics or tracking information

Legal Requirements

Since we do not collect or store user data on our servers, we have nothing to disclose in response to legal requests. If compelled by law enforcement or legal process, we can only provide publicly available information (app description, features, privacy policy).

Children's Privacy

DryveLyve is designed for adults who drive for business purposes and requires a driver's license to use legally. The app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Data Security

Your data security is maintained through:

  • Device-Only Storage: All data remains on your device under Android's security model
  • No Transmission: No opportunity for data interception since nothing is transmitted
  • Android Permissions: Other apps cannot access DryveLyve's data due to Android's app sandboxing
  • Encryption: Your device's built-in encryption protects app data when device is locked

Your Responsibility: Protect your device with a strong PIN/password/biometric lock. If your device is lost or stolen, your DryveLyve data is only as secure as your device lock screen.

Your Rights and Choices

Data Access

You have complete access to all your data through the app's interface. View, edit, or delete any trip, expense, or receipt at any time.

Data Export

Export your data anytime as CSV or PDF files through the Reports section. These files are saved to your device and can be shared as you choose.

Data Deletion

Delete individual trips, expenses, or receipts within the app, or uninstall the app to permanently delete all data from your device.

Permission Management

Manage app permissions through Android Settings > Apps > DryveLyve > Permissions. Note that revoking required permissions will prevent the app from functioning properly.
